# Sales Data Analysis Report

## Executive Summary
This report presents the analysis of sales data using Python and Pandas library.

## Key Findings

- **Total Revenue Generated**: ₹351,150.00
- **Average Sales per Transaction**: ₹43,893.75
- **Best Selling Product**: Laptop (₹222,000)
- **Total Items Sold**: 51

## Product-wise Sales Performance

| Product      | Total Sales (₹) | Percentage |
|--------------|------------------|----------|
| Laptop       | 222,000         | 63.22%  |
| Monitor      | 54,000          | 15.38%  |
| Headphones   | 28,000          | 7.97%   |
| Keyboard     | 27,900          | 7.95%   |
| Mouse        | 19,250          | 5.48%   |

## Key Insights

- Laptop is the **top revenue generator**, contributing over 63% of total sales.
- High-value products (Laptop & Monitor) are driving the majority of revenue.
- Small accessories (Mouse, Keyboard, Headphones) have high volume but lower contribution to revenue.
- There is significant growth potential in Monitor sales.

## Conclusion & Recommendations

The sales data clearly shows that focusing on high-value products like **Laptops** can significantly boost revenue. The company should:
- Continue promoting Laptop sales
- Explore strategies to increase Monitor sales
- Maintain steady supply of accessories
